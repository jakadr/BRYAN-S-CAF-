Our History

The story of Bryan's Cafe began in 1997, when coffee-loving lawyer bryan made a life-changing decision,

swapping legal books for coffee cups to follow his passion for his favourite brew.

Travelling to some of the most remote corners of the globe, bryan lived amongst the coffee-producing communities, experiencing first-hand the lifelong dedication and hard manual labour that went into growing their exquisite beans. Bryan knew that connoisseurs and everyday drinkers would love the specialty coffee as much as he did – but how to get the beans from crop to cafés?
